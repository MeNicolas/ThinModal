ThinModal

The simplest modal plugin in the world. Just add modal class on your link and your modal appear magically.

You can contribute at the project trough GitHub.

No style ? Not a problem.

Our modals haven't style. Why ? Like that, you can custom your modal like you want. If you wan't a default style, you can add the modalStyle class on your link. No more.

Examples

<a href="modal.html" class="modal">My modal</a>
